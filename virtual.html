How Does Virtual Testing Work?

We use the same clinical standards as in-person visits, but adapted for telehealth (Tele-ASD-Peds). This allows our clinicians to observe your child in their natural home environment in Murray or surrounding areas, often leading to more accurate results.

Benefits of Telehealth

  • Natural Environment: We see how your child acts at home, not in a scary doctor's office.

  • Convenience: No driving to a clinic. Log in from your home in Murray, Salt Lake, or anywhere in Utah.

  • Faster Answers: Virtual efficiency allows us to skip the 6-month waitlists common in Utah.

Signs & Symptoms by Age

Autism presents differently as a child grows. Use this guide to identify potential red flags.

Toddlers

12-24 Months
  • No babbling or pointing by age 1
  • Poor eye contact or smiling
  • Does not respond to name
  • Lines up toys instead of playing

Preschool

2-5 Years
  • Delayed speech / Echolalia
  • Difficulty playing "pretend"
  • Extreme tantrums over minor changes
  • Hand flapping or toe walking

School Age

6+ Years
  • Struggles to make or keep friends
  • Intense interest in specific topics
  • Misses social cues or sarcasm
  • Sensory sensitivities (loud noises)
